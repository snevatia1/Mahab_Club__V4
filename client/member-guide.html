Member Guide

  1. Pick a Check-in and Check-out on the calendar.
  2. Tap Show availability and add room(s).
  3. Use drop-downs for Adults 10+, Children 5–9, Infants 0–4.
  4. Enter Veg/Non-veg. (Totals must match guests, excluding infants.)
  5. If dates include BBQ nights, tick BBQ.
  6. Tap Proceed to Payment.
